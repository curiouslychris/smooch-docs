Sending action buttons

You can send your users an action button with the following syntax:

%[Button label here](http://anyurl.com)

This works from any channels (Slack, Helpscout, Email) you configured to use with SupportKit. Your action URL can be anything accepted by [[UIApplication sharedApplication] openURL:url]; So anything ranging from a web url, deep link into your app or app store link to ask for a review works like a charm.

Buttons will always be appended at the bottom of your message bubble.
